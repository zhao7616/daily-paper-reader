## 摘要
许多工程失效——如热点集中、赫兹接触疲劳局域化、边界层损失和混合死区——本质上都是几何失效模式：改变材料只能延缓失效，而改变几何形状则能消除失效。目前尚不存在用于评估凸体在滚动过程中表面接触分布均匀性的正式指标。我们引入了接触分布评分（CDS），即滚动表面接触时间分布的面积加权方差，以及其在压力域的对应指标——压力分布评分（SDS）。我们实现了一个三层预言机架构：近似、刚体和赫兹接触压力预言机。通过对可展滚动体族中 45 个成员的参数化搜索，确定了卵形体（Schatz, 1929）的 CDS 为 8.2e-7，SDS 为 8.1e-7，而圆柱体基准在两项指标上均差了 58 倍。0.98 的 SDS/CDS 比率表明，均匀的接触时间直接产生了均匀的接触压力。对疲劳（FDS）、热（TDS）和磨损（WDS）评分的扩展分析揭示了一个两层结构：一阶指标聚集在 8e-7 附近，而非线性疲劳指标由于 Basquin S-N 放大效应发散至 2.42e-6，但仍显示出卵形体的优越性。这项工作为基底几何（substrate geometry）建立了正式的词汇体系和计算基础设施，即研究由运行不变性分类的几何形状。

## Abstract
Many engineering failures thermal hotspot concentration, Hertz contact fatigue localization, boundary-layer loss, mixing dead zones are geometric failure modes: changing the material delays the failure; changing the geometry eliminates it. No formal metric exists for evaluating how uniformly a convex body distributes surface contact during rolling. We introduce the Contact Distribution Score (CDS), the area-weighted variance of contact time distribution over a rolling surface, and its stress-domain counterpart the Stress Distribution Score (SDS). We implement a three-layer oracle architecture: approximate, rigid-body, and Hertz contact pressure oracles. A parametric search over 45 members of the developable roller family identifies the oloid (Schatz, 1929) at CDS = 8.2e-7 and SDS = 8.1e-7, with the cylinder baseline 58x worse on both metrics. The SDS/CDS ratio of 0.98 demonstrates that uniform contact time directly produces uniform contact stress. Extended analysis across fatigue (FDS), thermal (TDS), and wear (WDS) scores reveals a two-tier structure: first-order metrics cluster near 8e-7 while the nonlinear fatigue metric diverges to 2.42e-6 due to Basquin S-N amplification, but still shows oloid superiority. This work establishes the formal vocabulary and computational infrastructure for substrate geometry the study of geometric forms classified by their operational invariants.
